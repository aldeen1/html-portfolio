The Best Movies of All Time

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Ultraman Rising

A new take of the ultraman series, in which the protagonist, Ken, An Ultraman, raises a baby Kaiju. The whole plot is heart warming and can perhaps make you cry. Recommend this movie if you like Ultraman and Kids.

Shin Ultraman

A more realistic version of Ultraman, giving a new feeling of sorts to the whole series. I dont really remember much of this movie but i can certaintly remember that this is a movie worth watching.

Spiderman Into the Spiderverse

Everyone have probably watched this one. Everyone likes spiderman, and now theres a bunch of em.
